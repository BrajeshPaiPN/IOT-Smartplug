About the ML Models
Real data · No synthetic generation

🧹 Cleaning Alert Model — Random Forest

Trained on real Indian solar plant data (2020, 34 days, ~68K readings). Detects dust soiling by comparing actual power output vs. expected output given irradiance levels. Features: irradiance, temperature, module temp, power ratio, time of day.

📉 Degradation Model — XGBoost Regressor

Trained on real Kerala Solar Power Dataset (2019–2024, 5 years of data). Computes efficiency ratio vs. Year-1 baseline and maps it to a degradation percentage. Features: irradiance, temperature, humidity, efficiency ratio, years in service.
